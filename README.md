# HR Attrition Analysis

## Objective
To analyze employee attrition patterns and identify key factors influencing employee turnover using the IBM HR Analytics dataset.

## Dataset
IBM HR Employee Attrition Dataset  
Total Records: 1470 Employees  

## Key Metrics
- Overall Attrition Rate: 16.12%
- Total Employees: 1470
- Employees Who Left: 237

## Analysis Conducted
- Attrition Distribution
- Attrition by Department
- Overtime vs Attrition
- Job Satisfaction vs Attrition

## Key Insights
- Approximately 1 in 6 employees leave the organization.
- The Sales department shows the highest attrition.
- Employees working overtime are more likely to leave.
- Lower job satisfaction levels are associated with higher attrition.

## Business Recommendations
- Reduce excessive overtime workload.
- Strengthen employee engagement initiatives.
- Focus retention strategies in high-risk departments.

## Tools Used
- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn
- Jupyter Notebook

## Conclusion
This analysis demonstrates how data-driven insights can support HR decision-making and improve employee retention strategies.
